Supply Chain Demand Forecasting Project

The web application developed using Streamlit offers an end-to-end supply chain demand forecasting solution with advanced time series forecasting capabilities and a user role identification feature, accompanied by crucial Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and relevant information. This intuitive and user-friendly platform enables supply chain professionals to make data-driven decisions efficiently.
